Max Temperature




Northern Hemisphere Max Temp vs. Latitude: There is a negative correlation between temperature and latitude in the northern hemisphere. As latitude decreases(cities that are closer to the equator), the maximum temperature increases.

Southern Hemisphere Max Temp vs. Latitude: There is a positive correlation between temperature and latitude in the southern hemisphere. As latitude increases(cities that are closer to the equator), the maximum temperature also increases.
